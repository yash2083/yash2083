Hey! I'm a **3rd-year CSE(Cyber Security) student at RV College of Engineering, Bengaluru** with a love for building things that are equal parts smart and useful. I bridge the gap between **security-first thinking** and **full-stack development** — from deploying RAG pipelines to edge AI fire detectors.

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🧠 [RAG KnowledgeFlow](https://github.com/yash2083/RAG-KnowledgeFlow)
> *Upload. Learn. Visualize. Repeat.*

A full-stack **RAG-powered adaptive learning platform** that transforms any document into a live, interactive knowledge graph. Ask questions, get cited answers, and watch your graph grow in real time — powered by **Groq's Llama 3.3 70B** at blazing speed.

`FastAPI` `React 18` `TypeScript` `Neo4j` `Qdrant` `PostgreSQL` `Redis` `Docker` `Groq` `Sentence-Transformers`

---

### 🏥 [Employee Health Monitoring System](https://github.com/yash2083)
> *Because health data deserves more than a spreadsheet.*

A full-stack web app for logging health records, calculating **cardiovascular risk scores**, and booking doctor appointments — complete with analytics dashboards and a clean relational DB schema.

`React.js` `Chart.js` `Flask` `SQLite` `Python`

---

### 📄 [Resume Screening App](https://github.com/yash2083/ResumeScreening)
> *Let the machine do the reading.*

Drag-and-drop your resume (PDF/DOCX/TXT), and an **NLP + SVM pipeline** instantly predicts which job category you belong to. Simple, fast, and surprisingly accurate.

`Python` `scikit-learn` `NLP` `Streamlit` `SVC`

---

### 🔥 [Fire Detection System — YOLOv5 + ESP32-CAM](https://github.com/yash2083/FireDetection)
> *Edge AI meets real-world safety.*

Real-time fire and smoke detection running on a **$5 ESP32-CAM** module. A Streamlit dashboard streams live detections and a Flask REST API fires alerts — all powered by a custom-trained YOLOv5 model.

`YOLOv5` `Python` `Streamlit` `Flask` `ESP32-CAM` `TFLite` `PlatformIO`

---

### ⚙️ [CPU Process Scheduler](https://github.com/yash2083/CPU-Process-Scheduler)
> *OS concepts, but make it visual.*

An interactive browser-based simulator for **Round Robin & Priority Scheduling** algorithms, complete with live Gantt chart generation and performance metrics. Great for OS students!

`HTML5` `Vanilla JavaScript` `Tailwind CSS`
